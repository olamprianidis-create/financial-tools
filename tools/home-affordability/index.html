Key Terms Explained
Gross Income
Your total income before taxes or any other deductions are taken out — this is the number lenders use when qualifying you for a loan.
PITI
Short for Principal, Interest, Taxes, and Insurance — the four costs that together make up your full monthly home payment.
Mortgage Principal
The original amount of money you borrowed from the bank to buy the home — every monthly payment chips away at this balance over time.
